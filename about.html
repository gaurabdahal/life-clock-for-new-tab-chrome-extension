Set Up Life Clock Extension

  • Open The Life Clock Extension From Extension bar by clicking on its icon
  • Inside the Pop Up, you will see a date input field where you can put your date of birth
  • Click on Submit button, and your date of birth will be saved.
  • Next time whenever you open a new tab, the current hours of your life, minutes and seconds will be displayed along with the random quote and background image from the flickr
